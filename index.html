About Us

Tarvie Community Alliance represents residents in Tarvie, Strathconon and surrounding communities who are affected by the proposed Tarvie Windfarm development.

Our goal is to ensure renewable energy developments are implemented responsibly, balancing environmental benefits with the needs and wellbeing of local communities.
